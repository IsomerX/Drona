Introducing Drona

Drona is a guiding robot which guides people around historic sites and spread awareness about the history and importance of the heritage sites.

It fly around you at an arms distance and fill you in with all the necessary details about the site you are seeing. It will be linked to your phone with either Wi-Fi or Bluetooth so that it maintains a strong connection with your phone

Has a surveillance system, to detect any mishappenings in the area using Real Time Object Detection and to send it the nearby police station.
Has an anti theft system to prevent people from stealing it.

Has a Built in High Resolution Camera to take your photos whenever and wherever you like. Has Wi-Fi/Bluetooth connectivity with your mobile up to a range of 5 meters.
